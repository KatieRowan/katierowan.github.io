I’m Katie, a UX/UI Designer in Washington, D.C.

Over the past decade, I've explored a wide variety of careers that have taken me from a law firm in Boston to a special needs private school in Maryland. Through it all, I’ve been driven by the motto, “Leave it better than you found it.”

Today, I design beautiful interfaces. More importantly, I design beautiful interfaces that work for all. I challenge assumptions, recognize my own biases, and put in the time to ensure designs are inclusive.

I’ve spent the past year and a half completing a design apprenticeship with Bloc, driving the design process from discovery & strategy through clickable prototypes. I’m a diligent planner, an empathetic researcher, and a creative visual designer.

When not working, I can be found reading science fiction and fantasy books, bouldering at Earth Treks, watching episodes of old Nickelodeon cartoons, or hiking with my dog Ziggy Stardust.
